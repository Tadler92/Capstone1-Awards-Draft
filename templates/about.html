What is the Awards Draft?

The Awards Draft is an annual draft (like Fantasy Football) for Movies. A group of drafters of any amount (you can have 2 or 20 if you wanted to)get together to draft 8 films each of any of the films released in the previous year that are up for Awards this year (so, in the year 2024, any film released to the public in 2023 can be drafted).

Once a group of drafters have selected their desired films, the points begin racking up. As films get nominated for certain awards and awards shows, they will receive the amount of points associated with that award show's award. To see the exact breakdown of Awards, Award Shows and Points, please see the "Points Breakdown" table below.

How did you come up with this idea?

I wish I could take credit for this idea (with as much as I love movies and watching the awards shows that honor them), but this is something that stemmed from a podcast I enjoyed listening to Box Office Party (when I learned about this podcast and this draft that they did, it was originally called the FMLPOD, but they eventually changed names because Fantasy Movie League shutdown during the COVID Pandemic...).

I enjoyed listening to their episodes in general, but also loved the fun and competitiveness that draft brought to their show, and I really wanted to do it with my friends. Honestly, I would've loved to have join them on the show doing the draft, but my friends are just as great, haha! So, I took their already laid out point system, and added a few more awards shows and points to it that way there could be a few more updates to the draft than just 3 shows announcing their nominees and winners and having them so spread apart from each other.

The first few years, I just kept track of everything on an Excel spreadsheet on my computer, then in 2023/2024, I upgraded to a website that my friends and I could access any time we wanted. The (eventual) final step is to make the website interactive and update automatically when a new nomination/win annoucement is made, but we're taking one step at a time!
